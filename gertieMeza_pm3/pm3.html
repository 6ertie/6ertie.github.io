Overview

There are five types of volcanoes: Shield, Cinder Cone, Composite Cone, Lava Plateau and Caldera. Of these five, the three most common volcanoes are: Shield, Cinder Cone and Composite Cone.

When a volcano erupts it emits hot water vaper, gases, lava, and rock fragments. Volcano types vary based on size, shape, mineral composition and eruptive behavior.
